Paylert Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 07/04/2026

Paylert ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This app is designed to provide UPI payment notification alerts and does not process or store any financial transactions.

1. Information We Access

Paylert requires access to your device's notifications in order to detect UPI payment messages and alert you.

2. How We Use Information

  • To detect incoming payment notifications
  • To trigger alerts for successful payments
We do NOT:
  • Store your personal messages
  • Access your bank account
  • Process any payments
  • Share your data with third parties

3. Data Storage

All data is processed locally on your device. We do not store your personal data on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as advertising providers (e.g., Google AdMob), which may collect limited data as per their own privacy policies.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure your data remains secure. However, no method is 100% secure.

6. User Control

You can disable notification access anytime from your device settings.

7. Changes to This Policy
